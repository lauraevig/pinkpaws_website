Our Mission

The mission of Pink Paws for the Cause is to help individuals with breast cancer both emotionally and financially with their diagnostic care and treatments. We will do this through the encouragement and support of the community and animal lovers.

Get Involved

We would love for you to help the cause! There are plenty of ways to get involved.

  • Create a local chapter: We have been very successful gathering a community of suppoerters through our Chicago chapter at a local high school. The students organize events and fund raisers while earning their required community service.
  • Donations: We are always happy to accept any donation you are able to make to our 501(c)3. We will provide you with a receipt.
  • Volunteer: If you ever want to help at an event, we are always looking for passionate volunteers!
