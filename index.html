Our Mission

The mission of Pink Paws for the Cause is to help individuals with breast cancer both emotionally and financially with their diagnostic care and treatments. We will do this through the encouragement and support of the community and animal lovers.

Get Involved

We would love for you to help the cause!
There are plenty of ways to get involved.

  • Volunteer: If you ever want to help at an event, we are always looking for passionate volunteers!
